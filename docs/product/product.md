# Product Vision — Dave Hajdu

**Boundary:** davehajdu.com is the founder's personal site within AI Officer LLC. Sister sites: ai-officer.com (cohort events + membership), caiocoach.com (coaching practice).

---

## What it is

A senior leader's personal platform — speaking, consulting, books, and writing. Three things happen here:

1. Convey credibility in three seconds.
2. Capture inquiries for keynotes and consulting.
3. Surface books and writing for readers and meeting bureaus.

The site's shape is a senior-leader résumé that converts. Conversion goes into the shared CRM as keynote, consultation, or general inquiries.

---

## Audiences

### 1. Speaker bureaus and event organisers

Looking to book the founder for a keynote or panel. They need: topic clarity, past stages, video clips, a quick contact path. Conversion is the keynote inquiry form.

### 2. Executives and leadership teams

Looking to engage the founder for AI strategy, advisory, or in-house workshops. They need: a credibility narrative, anchor topics (the four offices: Revenue, Talent, Operations, Innovation), and a low-friction inquiry form. Conversion is the consultation inquiry form.

### 3. Readers and audience

Curious about the founder's writing, books, or perspective. Lower commercial intent but compounds the brand. Conversion is reading the blog or buying a book.

---

## Values, ranked

1. **Credibility in three seconds.** The hero must convey what the founder does and why a senior buyer should engage.
2. **Frictionless inquiry.** Two clicks from any page to a form that fits the buyer's context.
3. **Long-form depth.** Articles and books are the proof behind the credibility cues.
4. **Cross-brand coherence.** Every inquiry lands in the shared CRM where it can compound across sister brands.

---

## Moat

- **Founder track record.** Real executive experience, citable past engagements.
- **Topic clarity.** The four offices (Revenue, Talent, Operations, Innovation) frame his perspective in a way buyers remember.
- **Cross-brand network.** A consultation inquiry here can convert into a coaching engagement at caiocoach.com or a retreat seat at ai-officer.com.

---

## Business model

| Channel | Pricing | Demand shape |
|---|---|---|
| Keynote | Bespoke | Bureau-driven, episodic |
| Consulting | Bespoke (5–6 figures) | Word-of-mouth, low volume, highest margin |
| Books | Per-unit (external retailer) | Low margin, audience-building |

The site is not a commerce surface. It is a credibility and capture surface that funnels into bespoke conversations.
